Alexa app customization for the smart mirror:

After installing "Amazon Alexa" app in your phone, you need to customize some things to meet your features on the smart mirror. These things are google calendar account, device location, time zone, measurement units and sound requests which are customized in the setting of the Alexa app.

Steps for customizing:

step 1

Open Alexa app, Sign-in with your amazon alexa account.

step 2

click"Device Settings", then selecting your device.

step 3

To set Time Zone: click on “Time Zone” then choose your zone.

step 4

To set Device location:click on “Device locations” then set your city and state/province/region.

step 5

To set Measurement Units:Click on "Measurement Units", then choose Celsius and choose the distance.

step 6

To set Sounds:Click "Sounds" and then turn on both buttons.They assure the user that Alexa is listening.

step 7

1-To link google calendar account:click “more”, then “setting” so:




2-Scroll down to “Calendar & Email” and click on “Accounts”, “Add Account”.






3-Finally choose your account to be set.
